Privacy Policy

Effective:

1. Introduction

Jetin ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our private jet charter services.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We may collect:

  • Contact details (name, email, phone number)
  • Passport/ID information for flight manifests
  • Payment and billing information
  • Travel preferences and special requirements

2.2 Technical Data

When you visit our website, we may collect:

  • IP address and browser type
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Cookie data (see our Cookies Policy)

3. How We Use Your Data

Purpose Legal Basis
Flight booking and operations Contractual necessity
Payment processing Legal obligation
Customer service Legitimate interest
Marketing communications Consent

4. Data Sharing

We only share data when necessary with:

  • Aviation operators and crew
  • Immigration and customs authorities
  • Payment processors
  • Service providers under strict confidentiality

5. International Transfers

As a global service, your data may be transferred internationally. We ensure all transfers comply with applicable data protection laws.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard measures including:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Secure access controls
  • Regular security audits

7. Your Rights

Under GDPR and other privacy laws, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Object to processing
  • Withdraw consent
  • Lodge complaints with authorities

8. Policy Changes

We may update this policy periodically. Significant changes will be notified via email or website notice.
