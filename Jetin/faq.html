JetIn Private Jet Charter FAQ

Expert answers to your most common private aviation questions

What types of private jets are available for charter?

JetIn offers a diverse fleet of luxury private aircraft:

  • Light Jets: Ideal for short trips (4-6 passengers, range: 1,500-2,000 miles)
  • Midsize Jets: Perfect balance of comfort and performance (6-8 passengers, range: 2,500-3,000 miles)
  • Super Midsize Jets: Enhanced range with premium amenities (8-10 passengers, range: 3,500-4,000 miles)
  • Large Cabin Jets: Spacious luxury for extended travel (10-16 passengers, range: 6,000+ miles)
  • Ultra-Long Range Jets: Non-stop intercontinental capability

Our fleet includes top manufacturers like Gulfstream, Bombardier, Dassault, and Embraer.

How much does it cost to charter a private jet?

Private jet charter costs vary based on several factors:

  • Aircraft Type: From $2,500/hr for light jets to $10,000+/hr for large cabin jets
  • Flight Distance: Longer flights typically have better hourly rates
  • Positioning Fees: If the aircraft needs to relocate for your flight
  • Additional Services: Catering, ground transport, special requests
  • International Fees: Landing rights, customs, handling fees

For an exact quote, please provide your itinerary details to our flight specialists.

Can I travel with my pet on a private jet?

Absolutely! Most JetIn aircraft can accommodate pets with advance notice:

  • No cargo hold - your pet travels in the cabin with you

Please inform us about your pet's breed, size, and any special needs when booking.

What are empty leg flights and how can I save with them?

Empty leg flights offer significant savings when aircraft need to reposition:

  • Savings: Typically 30-75% off standard charter rates
  • Availability: Routes vary daily based on aircraft movements
  • Flexibility: Must be adaptable with timing as flights can change
  • Booking: Often available with short notice

Contact our team for current empty leg opportunities that may match your travel needs.
